## Salt by mortuusars

Source: `mortuusars/Salt`  
License file distributed by the source project: `LICENSE.md` (MIT license text)

Create: Gravlax includes code and adapted assets derived from the salt system
of **Salt**, ported and integrated into the salt-processing branch. This
includes salt-related implementation and visual resources for:

- Salt and Raw Rock Salt;
- Rock Salt Ore and related underground generation;
- Salt Block, Budding Salt Block, salt buds and Salt Cluster;
- Salt Lamp;
- related supporting assets used by the integrated salt line.

A copy of the source project's `LICENSE.md` text is bundled in:
`src/main/resources/META-INF/create_gravlax/licenses/SALT_MIT_LICENSE.md`.
